OUR STORY

"Our Friendly Team Works Hard to Bring You Real Indian Food and Make You Feel Right at Home"

"At Indifeast, our story starts with a big dream: to share the wonderful flavors of India with everyone in the UK. Our founder loved Indian food so much that they wanted to create a special place where people could come and enjoy real Indian dishes. We opened our doors in 1990 and quickly became a favorite spot for anyone looking to taste true Indian cooking. Our main idea is "Hot and Tasty Tradition," which means we’re all about serving delicious food that feels like a warm hug, just like the dishes made in homes across India."

Walking into Indifeast is like stepping into a piece of India, with a cozy and welcoming setting that makes everyone feel at home. Our team treats every guest like family, making sure each visit is filled with joy and tasty food. We're proud of using traditional recipes and the best ingredients to make sure every dish is full of flavor and tells the story of India's rich food culture. From the moment you smell the spices in the air to the last bite of your meal, Indifeast offers a journey of discovery and delight, letting you explore the true taste of Indian cuisine in the most enjoyable way

Our Mission

Alongside serving authentic Indian dishes, we are dedicated to supporting our community by feeding the homeless. For us, it's about more than just food; it's about sharing warmth and care with everyone. Every meal you enjoy at Indifeast helps us provide hot meals to people facing hard times. This commitment to give back is a big part of who we are, bringing the spirit of Indian hospitality to not just our customers, but to those in our community who need it the most.
